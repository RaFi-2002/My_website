Introduction to Wood Science and Technology

2024 • Collaborative Work • Featured Publication

Focus on wood-based composite chemistry, processing, and applications. This comprehensive study explores innovative approaches to sustainable wood technology.

This comprehensive publication presents cutting-edge research in wood science and technology, with particular emphasis on wood-based composite chemistry. The work covers innovative processing techniques, sustainable applications, and future directions in the field. Our research contributes to the understanding of molecular interactions in wood composites and provides practical solutions for industrial applications.

Keywords: Wood composites, Sustainable technology, Material science, Chemical processing

Enzymatic Bioreactor Optimization Using Machine Learning

2024 • Bioprocess Engineering • Best Paper Award

Advanced predictive models developed using MATLAB and Python to enhance enzyme production yield in bioreactors with AI-driven optimization techniques.

This research presents novel predictive models developed using MATLAB and Python to enhance enzyme production yield in bioreactors. By implementing machine learning algorithms and optimization techniques, we achieved a 35% improvement in efficiency and 28% increase in output compared to traditional methods.

The study demonstrates the successful integration of computational tools with bioprocess engineering, providing a framework for real-time optimization and control of bioreactor systems.

Impact Factor: 4.2 • Citations: 45+

Advanced Membrane Technologies for Industrial Wastewater Treatment

2023 • Environmental Engineering • International Journal

Novel membrane separation techniques for treating complex industrial effluents with enhanced selectivity and energy efficiency.

This study investigates advanced membrane technologies for treating complex industrial wastewater streams. We developed novel composite membranes with enhanced selectivity and reduced energy consumption, achieving 95% contaminant removal efficiency.

The research addresses critical environmental challenges and provides sustainable solutions for industrial water treatment, contributing to circular economy principles.

Collaborators: International research consortium • Funding: NSF Grant
